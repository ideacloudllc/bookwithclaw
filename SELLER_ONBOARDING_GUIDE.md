## Step 2: Complete Your Hotel Profile

After signup, you'll see this form. **Fill it out completely** (takes 3 minutes).

### Required Information

**Basic Info**
- Hotel name (exact name)
- Address (street, city, state, ZIP)
- Phone number (guest inquiries go here)
- Website (if you have one)
- Contact email (your email, not guest-facing)

**Hotel Details**
- Total rooms (how many rooms you have)
- Star rating (2-5 stars, or "unrated")
- Established year (when you opened)
- Check-in/checkout times (e.g., 3pm / 11am)

**Policies**
- Cancellation policy (flexible, moderate, strict)
- Minimum stay (e.g., 1 night, 2 nights, etc.)
- Advance booking (e.g., must book 7 days ahead)

**Add Photos**
- Hotel exterior (1-3 photos)
- Lobby/common area (1-2 photos)
- Sample guest room (1-2 photos)
- **Why?** Buyers see these and trust you more. Professional photos = higher booking rates.

**Pricing Info** (This is not final; you'll set rates per room)
- Average nightly rate (e.g., $250-400)
- Seasonal variation (summer vs. winter premium)
- Group booking discount? (e.g., 10% off for 5+ rooms)

**Preferences**
- Who can negotiate? (You/revenue manager/auto-accept)
- Notification method (email, SMS, in-app)
- Preferred booking languages (English, Spanish, etc.)


**Photos** (Very important!)
- Room overview (1)

- **Minimum:** 2 photos. **Ideal:** 4-5 photos.

### Pricing & Availability

**Base Rate**
```
Monday-Thursday:  $250
Friday-Sunday:    $350
```

*This is what you'd accept in a negotiation. AI agents will negotiate down slightly from here.*

**Floor Price** (Minimum you'll accept)
```
$200
```
*If a buyer offers less than this, deal is rejected automatically.*

**Availability**
- [ ] Open from today
- [ ] Closed certain dates? (Add blocked dates)
- Example: "Closed Dec 23-26, blocked for maintenance May 1-7"

**Lead Time** (How far in advance do you want bookings?)
```
Minimum: 2 days advance
Maximum: 180 days advance
```
*Buyers can only book within this window.*

### Negotiation Preferences

**Let AI negotiate?**
- [ ] Yes, auto-negotiate within my ranges
- [ ] No, send me all offers for manual review

**Acceptable terms** (What matters to you?)
- [ ] Price (primary)
- [ ] Length of stay (3+ nights = better for cleaning?)
- [ ] Booking date (advance = more planning time)
- [ ] Repeat customer (loyalty discount?)

**Example negotiation:**
```
Buyer intent: "I want a Deluxe King for 2 nights (March 22-24) at $400 total"
Base rate: $350/night = $700 total
Floor price: $200/night = $400 total
AI agent: "We can do $330/night ($660 total) if you book 7 days in advance"
```

**Click:** "Create Room"

## Step 4: Connect Your Stripe Account

### Navigate to: Settings → Payment → Connect Stripe

**Why Stripe?**
- Industry standard (PCI-compliant)
- Fastest payouts (2 business days)
- Transparent fees (we charge 1.8%, not hidden)
- Your customers' cards never touch us

### The Process (3 minutes)

1. Click **"Connect Stripe Account"**
2. You'll go to Stripe's site (stripe.com)
3. Enter:
   - Business name (your hotel name)
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Bank account (where we deposit payouts)
   - Tax ID (EIN for US, equivalent for your country)
4. Stripe verifies (instant for most)
5. You're connected! 🎉

**Payment Flow:**
```
Buyer pays → Stripe holds funds (48 hours) → Settlement confirmed 
→ Stripe deposits to your bank (2 business days)
→ You see money in your account
```

**Fee breakdown** (for a $450 booking):
```
Booking value:              $450.00
BookWithClaw fee (1.8%):      -$8.10
Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30):   -$13.40
Your payout:               $428.50
```

## Best Practices for Success

### 1. Photos Matter
- Use natural lighting (not dark/blurry)
- Show room cleanliness, beds, bathrooms clearly
- Include a view or unique feature
- **Result:** 3-5 good photos = 2x more bookings

### 2. Set Realistic Prices
- Research your market (Booking.com, Airbnb, Google rates)
- Undercut OTA rates by 5-10% for direct bookings
- Remember: OTA charges 12%, you're only paying 1.8%
- Example: If Booking.com shows $300, you can accept $270

### 3. Respond Quickly
- Check your offers 2-3x daily
- Auto-negotiate is faster than manual (if you're comfortable)
- The faster you respond, the higher close rate

### 4. Be Clear on Policies
- Cancellation terms (strict? flexible?)
- Early check-in/late checkout (available?)
- Special requests (cribs, pets, etc.)
- **Clarity = fewer disputes**

### 5. Monitor Your Metrics
- **Click-through rate** (% of viewers who inquire) - target: 5-10%
- **Conversion rate** (% of inquiries that close) - target: 30-50%
- **Average negotiated rate** vs. your floor price
- **Payout per booking** (after fees)

## Troubleshooting

### "I'm not getting any offers"
1. Check your pricing (too high vs. market?)
2. Add more/better photos
3. Make sure Stripe is connected
4. Contact us – we can feature your listing

### "Stripe keeps rejecting my payout"
1. Verify bank account details are correct
2. Make sure account matches business name
3. Check routing number (it's on your check)
4. Contact Stripe support if still stuck

### "I want to reject an offer"
1. Go to Offers & Negotiations
2. Click the offer
3. Click "Decline" with optional message
4. Offer is rejected, no negotiations happen

### "I want to change my pricing"
1. Go to Rooms → Edit room
2. Update base rate, floor price, or features
3. Click "Save"
4. Changes apply to future offers immediately

### "I want to add another manager"
1. Go to Settings → Team
2. Click "Invite Manager"
3. Enter their email
4. They'll receive an invite to join
