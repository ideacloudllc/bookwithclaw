## 1. Target Audience & Positioning

### Primary Targets (Tier 1)
**Profile:** Independent boutique hotels, 10-50 rooms, 60-80% occupancy

- **Why them:** Tech-friendly, margin-conscious, direct booking priority
- **Pain point:** OTA fees (12-15%), booking platform clutter, need better rate control
- **Value prop:** "Direct bookings with AI negotiation. 1.8% vs. 12% commissions. Full rate control."

### Secondary Targets (Tier 2)
**Profile:** Small hotel chains (5-20 properties), group booking platforms

- **Why them:** Scale, operational efficiency, volume drivers
- **Pain point:** Batch bookings, complex negotiations, rate management across properties
- **Value prop:** "Automate room negotiations across all properties. Faster deals, better rates."

### Tier 3 (Stretch)
**Profile:** Vacation rentals, Airbnb hosts with multiple properties

- **Why them:** Direct bookings, high-margin, tech-savvy
- **Pain point:** Inconsistent pricing, manual inquiries, commission drag
- **Value prop:** "Smart pricing + automated negotiation. Never leave money on table."

## 3. Direct Outreach Sequence

### Phase 1: Research & List Building (1 day)
- Identify 50 hotels (boutique, independent, tech-forward)
- Compile contact info (GM email, booking manager, owner LinkedIn)
- Segment by tier, property size, current OTA strategy
- **Sources:**
  - Google Maps (boutique hotels + independent hotels filter)
  - Booking.com reviews → filter by "smaller properties"
  - LinkedIn (hotel general managers + revenue managers)
  - Industry directories (Small Luxury Hotels, Design Hotels, etc.)

### Phase 2: Cold Email Campaign (2 days)
**Sequence:** Email 1 → 2-day wait → Email 2 (follow-up) → 3-day wait → Phone call

**Email 1: Hook** (Subject line tests below)
```
Subject: [Test 1] Cut OTA fees in half? (boutique hotels in {city})
Subject: [Test 2] {Hotel} + direct bookings = $XX extra monthly
Subject: [Test 3] Ask us how we're saving hotels 10K/month on commissions
```

**Email 2: Social proof** (if no response)
- Mention 1-2 hotels already signed up (once they exist)
- Add calendar link for 15-min intro call
- Time-sensitive angle: "First 10 hotels get setup fee waived"

### Phase 3: Warm Outreach (Phone + Video) (2 days)
**Script:** "Hi {Name}, I saw {Hotel} has great reviews. We help independent hotels like you do more direct bookings at better rates. 15-min call to see if it's a fit?"

## 6. Pricing & Incentives for Sellers

### Positioning
```
Standard: 1.8% per booking (flat, no surprises)
Startup incentive: 0.9% for first 30 days (then 1.8%)
Volume discount: 1.5% if >100 bookings/month
```

### Why 1.8%?
- **Market:** OTAs charge 12-15% (we're 87% cheaper)
- **Sustainable:** $450 hotel booking × 1.8% = $8.10 revenue
- **Competitive:** Stripe + ops + liquidity costs ~0.5%, leaves 1.3% margin
- **Messaging:** "1.8% beats any OTA. Ever."

## 11. FAQ for Sellers (Preempt Objections)

**Q: Why would I use BookWithClaw instead of my OTA?**
A: You wouldn't replace them. This complements OTAs by handling direct bookings better—and the 1.8% fee is 87% cheaper than Booking.com.

**Q: What if BookWithClaw shuts down?**
A: Your bookings are direct—we're just the negotiation layer. You control pricing and availability.

**Q: How long until I get paid?**
A: Within 2 business days of settlement. Stripe handles the transfer.

**Q: Can I negotiate rates with buyers myself?**
A: Yes! You set floor prices and preferred terms. AI agents negotiate within those bounds. You stay in control.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: All payment data handled by Stripe (PCI-compliant). We never see credit cards. Full encryption.
