```
Better buyer pool = more bookings per hotel = more transactions for us.

We win when you win.
```

**"Isn't that the same as my website?"**
```
Good instinct! Your website requires buyers to call you directly, 
negotiate back-and-forth, figure out payment, etc.

We skip the friction:
- Buyers submit intents directly (no phone tag)
- AI agents negotiate terms (you don't have to)
- Payments handled automatically (Stripe)
- You get notified when a deal closes

It's the negotiation layer between you and buyers.
```

**"I'm already using Booking.com. Do I have to switch?"**
```
Nope! Use both. Booking.com for scale/discovery. 
BookWithClaw for margin/direct bookings.

Think of it as a new channel alongside your OTAs, not instead of.

More bookings, lower commission. Best of both worlds.
```

**"When would we start seeing bookings?"**
```
Usually within 24-48 hours of going live. 
We have buyers already looking for rooms like yours.

You'd be live in 10 minutes. First offer within a few hours, probably.

```

```
You just:
1. Add your rooms (drag-drop photos)
2. Set a floor price
3. Turn AI negotiation on
```
